## BIOGRAPHY
**[taomengling](https://github.com/taomengling)** is currently a postgraduate in Wuhan National Laboratory for Optoelectronics (**[WNLO](http://english.wnlo.hust.edu.cn/)**), Huazhong University of Science and Technology (**[HUST](http://english.hust.edu.cn/)**), Wuhan, China. Prior to his Ph.D. journey, he received the B.S. degree from Computer Science and Technology, Northeast Forestry University (**[NEFU](http://en.nefu.edu.cn/)**), Heilongjiang, China, in 2016. His current main research interests include cloud storage systems, cache theory, non-volatile memory devices, etc. He has published papers in international conferences and journals including USENIX ATC, DAC, DATE, ICCD, the IEEE Transactions on Parallel
and Distributed Systems, etc.
